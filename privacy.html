Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2024

Introduction

TeamBalance is a client-side web application for managing team rosters and player ratings. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

All your data (players, ratings, teams, activities) is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.

Google Sheets Integration

TeamBalance offers optional integration with Google Sheets to export and import player data. When you use this feature:

  • You explicitly authorize the app to access your Google Sheets
  • The app requests only the minimum necessary permissions (spreadsheets scope)
  • Data is transmitted directly between your browser and Google's servers
  • We do not store your Google account credentials or access tokens
  • You can revoke access at any time through the app or your Google Account settings

Data We Access

When you use Google Sheets integration, the app can:

  • Read data from spreadsheets you specify
  • Write player data to spreadsheets you specify
  • Create new spreadsheets in your Google Drive (only when you explicitly request export to a new spreadsheet)

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies for tracking purposes
  • Any data from your Google account beyond the spreadsheets you explicitly share

Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Identity Services and Google Sheets API for the optional integration feature. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on how Google handles your data.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser
  • You can export your data at any time
  • You can delete all data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • You can disconnect Google Sheets integration at any time

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
